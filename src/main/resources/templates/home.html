Welcome to Cat Family!





Do u like cats?


CatFamily is a simple Spring Boot application that helps you keep track of your customers and cats. With CatFamily, you can easily add new customers and cats by filling in simple forms, and view all of your records on separate pages. If you're a cat lover or you own a business related to cats, CatFamily can help you stay organized and keep track of all of your important information. Whether you're a breeder, a pet store owner, or simply a cat enthusiast, CatFamily can make your life easier. So why not give CatFamily a try? It's easy to use, and it's completely free and open source. Plus, if you're a developer, you can even contribute to the project and help make it even better!
